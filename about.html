Galleria Commedia

Tristan Van Garsse

Hi, my name is Tristan and I am a Belgian graphic designer who has an infuriating tendency to draw on every little piece of paper I can find.

Here's why I created this website:

Popular social media apps like Instagram, Twitter and Facebook offer many advantages for sharing one's creative work. Lately however, I realized they shifted their focus from building a community of creative people to turning intelligent people into mindless drones. Images and words are treacherous because they can convey anything, and they can - combined with corporate stakes - very easily turn your app into an addiction. I therefore decided that I needed my own space for sharing my art with the world and built this little website as a result.
